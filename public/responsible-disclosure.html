Responsible Disclosure

Help us maintain a secure platform

Our Commitment to Security

We value the security of our platform and appreciate the efforts of security researchers in helping us maintain a secure environment. This policy outlines our approach to responsible disclosure of security vulnerabilities.

Disclosure Guidelines

What to Report

  • Security vulnerabilities
  • Data exposure risks
  • Authentication issues
  • Cryptographic weaknesses
  • Access control problems

What Not to Report

  • UI/UX improvements
  • Feature requests
  • Performance issues
  • Non-security bugs
  • Third-party services

Disclosure Process

Steps to Follow

  1. Identify the vulnerability
  2. Document your findings
  3. Submit via secure channel
  4. Allow time for assessment
  5. Coordinate on fix timeline
  6. Wait for public disclosure

Safe Harbor

Our Promise

  • No legal action for good-faith testing
  • Recognition for valid reports
  • Transparent communication
  • Fair assessment process

How to Report

Required Information

  • Vulnerability description
  • Steps to reproduce
  • Potential impact
  • Proof of concept
  • Suggested fix (optional)

Our Response

What to Expect

  • Initial response within 24 hours
  • Regular status updates
  • Clear communication
  • Recognition for valid reports

Disclosure Timeline

Process Steps

  • Report submission
  • Initial assessment
  • Fix development
  • Testing and verification
  • Public disclosure

Recognition

How We Acknowledge

  • Security Hall of Fame
  • Public acknowledgments
  • Bug bounty rewards
  • Special mentions

Submit a Report

Contact Us

To report a security vulnerability, please email us at security@steganocrypt.com with the subject line "Security Vulnerability Report".

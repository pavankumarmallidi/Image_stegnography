Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

Introduction

At SteganoCrypt, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Contact information (email address)
  • Messages you choose to hide in images
  • Images you upload for processing

Automatically Collected Information

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • IP address
  • Usage statistics

How We Use Your Information

Service Provision

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To process your steganography requests
  • To improve our service

Communication

  • To respond to your inquiries
  • To send important updates

Data Security

  • No storage of processed images or messages

  • Regular security audits and updates
